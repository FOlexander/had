HOW TO

Here you can receive two types of reports. The first report shows you the average retention period for your company overall. The second one shows the retention period for two groups with an explanation, is there any meaningful difference between these groups. If your aim is to receive a valid report, you should prepare a valid data table, please follow the instructions below to prepare your data.


To get an average retention period report for your company, please create an Excel file with columns. The first column is called "StartDay'' and you must put the date of your employee's first workday here. The second column is called "LastDay'' and shows the date of your employee's last workday. You should leave it blank if the employee is still working. Then, download the Excel file in the form below. After that, in a few seconds, you will see a report of the retention period with explanations.

To get the retention period for the two groups with an explanation, you have to make the same Excel file, but add a third column with any attribute that has two options, for example, gender(female, male), education (college, high school ), mentoring program participation (yes, no). Then upload the file, and you will see a report with explanations!
